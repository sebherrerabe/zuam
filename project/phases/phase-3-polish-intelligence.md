# Phase 3 Polish And Intelligence

## Goal
Add prioritization intelligence, explainable insights, positive-only player progression, animation polish, notes conversion, dashboards, and saved-view maturity.

## Included Modules
- `analytics-insights`
- `player-progression-rewards`

## Entry Dependencies
- Phase 2 interaction and data contracts are stable enough to support new scoring and reporting features.

## Exit Criteria
- Scoring and scheduling contract boundaries are specified.
- Reporting and progression semantics are split into separate modules with explicit interfaces.
- Cross-platform telemetry expectations are specified.
